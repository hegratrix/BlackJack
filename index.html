How To Play 420 BlackJack...

Since I built this based on an open source BlackJack code... where I could only figure out to display up to four cards to each side... without breaking the design... I had to make up a few new rules to work with this limitation... with that...

Here are the rules to win 420 BlackJack:

  1. You have full control over both Hand 1 and Hand 2... with no down card... meaning you see everything...hell... I even provided a card counting tool in the upper left corner of the game board...
  2. As stated above both Hands will be dealt a maximum of 4 cards...
  3. Hand 2 MUST equal at least 16 points when the card max of 4 is met... not doing so will result in a push for a busted Hand 1... or a win for any Hand 1 equalling 21 and under...
  4. As a bonus... double dealt natural BlackJacks equal double the points...
  5. Ultimately... Hand 1 MUST beat a qualified Hand 2 using traditional BlackJack rules... based on your decisions for both hands...
  6. Play... and play again... until the quirks of the game become clear to you!!!
